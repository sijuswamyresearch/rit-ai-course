Foundation of Applied AI & Machine Learning

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology (RIT), Kottayam

A comprehensive 20-hour intensive course on Applied AI and Machine Learning designed for engineering students from all disciplines. Covers mathematical foundations, classical ML, deep learning, NLP with transformers, and deployment with Streamlit. Includes hands-on projects and capstone implementation.

Author

Siju Swamy

Keywords

machine learning, artificial intelligence, deep learning, natural language processing, transformers, neural networks, python, data science, AI course, RIT Kottayam, engineering education, OBE curriculum

Welcome to the Course

This 20-hour intensive foundation course transitions engineering students from fundamental mathematics to deploying modern Generative AI applications.

Pre-flight Checklist for Students

Before our first session, please ensure you have completed the following:

  1. GitHub Account: Create a free account at github.com.
  2. Kaggle Account: Register at kaggle.com to access our datasets.
  3. Google Colab: Ensure you can access Google Colab using your personal or university email.
